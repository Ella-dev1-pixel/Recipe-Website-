Chicken Shawarma

Simple • Smoky • Bold

Ingredients Steps Tips Contact

Crispy edges, tender inside — Pure comfort

What is shawarma?

Middle Eastern street food royalty. Traditionally stacked on a vertical spit, our oven version brings the same aromatic spices, juicy chicken, and that irresistible char .

📦 Ingredients

For The Chicken

  • 2 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Juice from 1 lemon
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 big onion, sliced

Yogurt Sauce (Toum style)

  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • Juice from ½ lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes

To Serve

  • Pita bread (warm)
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Tomato wedges
  • Thin cucumber slices

🔥 Step by Step

1

Marinate deeply

Combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and all spices. Coat chicken thighs thoroughly. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours (overnight = even better).

2

Prep & Bake

Heat oven to 425°F. Add sliced onions to the marinade, then spread everything on a lined baking sheet.

3

Roast To Golden

Bake for 28–30 minutes until edges are caramelized and chicken is cooked through. Rest 5 minutes, then slice thin against the grain.

4

Whisk the Sauce

While chicken bakes: mix yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt + red pepper flakes. Set aside.

5

Assemble & Crush

Warm pita, layer sliced chicken, onions, fresh veggies, drizzle with garlicky yogurt sauce. Wrap and enjoy.

⚡ Pro Tips

Customize it

Add pickled turnips, hot sauce, or crispy fries inside — shawarma is a blank canvas.

Storage Hack

Keep chicken and sauce separate in airtight containers. Stays delicious for up to 4 days.

Beyond the Pita

Serve over basmati rice, on a salad bowl, or with roasted veggies. Ultimate meal prep.
